About Us

Background

Phenduka

Phenduka is a black-owned company that offers management consulting and training services. The company provides services to clients across South Africa. The company was founded by professionals with with many years of consulting experience. This experience, combined with a deep understanding of organizational change management issues, places Phenduka in a unique position to deliver successful change initiative.

Leadership & Coaching

Our clients work in an ever-changing world. Changing priorities,changing landscape, human resources processes, and technology needs.Managers and supervisors need coaching skills to support their users and staff go through chanage. Phenduka will assist in coaching your managers through the change.

Change Management

When your organization undertakes projects or initiatives to improve performance, seize opportunities or address key issues, they often require changes; changes to the process, job roles, organizational structures, and types and uses of technology. We will help you manage the change through a structure and systemic process.

Project Execution & Leadership

Work with our team to increase your chances of realizing promised benefits through an integrated approach. We believe that a great strategy does not just implement itself. Our strategy encompasses the best practices so that our experienced consultants can bring to bear in ensuring that the aspiration of our clients is realized. Phenduka has assembled a group of highly skilled professionals with outstanding track records in project delivery.
