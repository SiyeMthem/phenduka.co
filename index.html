Phenduka means change, our company will support your change initiative by creating customised change management
approaches and solutions for your needs, as well as equip your company to gain a solid understanding of change
at an individual and organisational level, as a valuable skill of the future

Services We Offer

Premium Services provided by Phenduka Management.

Leadership & Coaching

Our clients work in an ever-changing world. Changing priorities, changing landscape, human resources processes, and technology needs. Managers and supervisors need coaching skills to support their users and staff go through change.

Change Management

Phenduka will support the design, development, delivery and management of communications in order to align users to the change initiative.

Project Execution & Leadership

We will work with your team to increase your chances of realizing promised benefits through an integrated approach. We believe that a great strategy does not just implement itself. Our strategy encompasses the best practices so that our experienced consultants can bring to bear in ensuring that the aspiration of our clients is realized.

Stakeholder Engagement

Our stakeholder management strategy ensures that stakeholders are correctly identified, correctly aligned to the change initiative and supported throughout the implementation.

Business User Engagement and Readiness

We believe that the workforce or ‘users’ are key to any change initiative, therefore it is critical that they are ready to manage the change beyond the project timeline. Phenduka will put in place user readiness efforts to ensure the workforce is ready to realise the value of the change initiative.
